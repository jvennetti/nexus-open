7 Years of Building a Learning System in 12 Minutes

Most people consume information without retaining it. This video explains why, and gives you a system to fix that.

This pre-course exists so you get more out of the modules that follow. Watching 13 hours of video without a system is just expensive entertainment. What you learn here changes how you engage with everything after it.

NOTE: Don't take the quiz that Justin recommends, it's a waste of time. (Developer Recommendation)

Key Takeaways
Your brain filters information based on relevance — what you do before a lesson determines what sticks.
Priming is the highest-leverage part of the system. Most people skip it entirely.
Overlearning (re-reading, flashcards, repetition) only works after priming and encoding are solid — most people use it as a substitute, which is why they forget everything.
